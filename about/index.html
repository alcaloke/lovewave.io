About - What we do

We are a multi-disciplinary collective, creating hardware and software products for enhancing the user's sensory perception.

We design and make interactive and immersive products with the purpose of evoking the user's potential to fully sense color, sound and motion. We achieve this by...

Our mission is for the users of our products to develop their sensibility, creativity and empathy, by becoming more aware of the sensory enviroment in which they live their lives.

Our first product Sound Mirror is a digitally updated Color Organ (a radioshack product from the 70s); featuring a LED light display that responds beautifully to music or any sound that is played in its proximity.
